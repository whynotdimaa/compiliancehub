# Data Protection Policy

## Purpose and Scope

This policy governs how Demo Corp collects, processes, retains and deletes
personal data across all products and internal systems. It applies to all
employees, contractors and processors acting on behalf of the company.

## Data Retention

Personal data is retained for **five years** after the end of the customer
relationship. After the retention period expires, data is deleted or
irreversibly anonymized within 30 days. Retention clocks are tracked per
record category in the processing register.

Backups containing personal data follow the same retention schedule; expired
backup sets are destroyed as part of the monthly rotation.

## Records of Processing (GDPR Article 30)

In line with GDPR Article 30, the company maintains a record of processing
activities. The record includes, for every processing activity:

- the purposes of the processing,
- categories of data subjects and categories of personal data,
- categories of recipients, including processors and third countries,
- the envisaged retention periods,
- a general description of technical and organisational security measures.

The record is reviewed quarterly by the Data Protection Officer.

## Data Exports

Only **administrators** may export customer records from production systems.
Every export is logged with the user identity, timestamp, and the scope of
the exported data. Export logs are retained for three years and reviewed
monthly by the security team.

## Data Subject Rights

Requests for access, rectification or erasure are handled within 30 days.
Erasure requests override the standard retention schedule unless a legal
obligation requires continued storage.
